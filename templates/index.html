·COLD CALLS(On boarding):

Cold calls are a very important part of the after sales process and are a good way to sell. This is how cold calls should be made: CS Agent: “Hello Eva, this is Sandra calling in from Safeboda.” Agent must call the customer by name, and these names are known because they already exist in a database. This will help keep the attention of the customer as opposed to talking to a stranger.
